Sponsor a Woman's Menstrual Health

Since our inception, Rigel Foundation has been working extensively across multiple states in India to eliminate period poverty and build a stigma-free environment for women and girls.

We work in close coordination with local authorities, schools, and self-help groups to ensure every woman has access to safe, hygienic menstrual products and accurate biological education.

You can help us achieve this vision by choosing to donate. Your contribution directly funds hygiene kits containing sanitary pads, disposal mechanisms, and educational booklets.

We urge you to play your part in gifting health and dignity. By choosing to donate online, your contributions can help us reach more women and support their fundamental needs.
